Who I Am

About Me

I am a cybersecurity and information systems audit professional with an MSc in Information Technology & Applied Security (specialisation: Computer Networking & Cybersecurity) from Islington College — London Metropolitan University, completed 2025.

Recognised as Employee of the Year 2024 (March 12, 2024) by Managing Director Narayan Koirala at Eminence Ways Pvt. Ltd — "Your Cybersecurity Partner" — I single-handedly led IS audit engagements across Class A banks, insurance companies, ISPs, IT firms, and government bodies, coordinating opening/closing meetings with boards and delivering ISO 27001:2013 & 2022 aligned audit reports.

My technical arsenal spans ISO 27001, GDPR, NIST, CIS Benchmarks, Palo Alto, CrowdStrike, Sophos, Azure, Active Directory, Windows Server 2016/2019/2022, Hyper-V, VMware, Veeam, and network monitoring via Nagios and iMaster NCE — giving me both the governance depth and hands-on infrastructure credibility rare in the security profession.

I hold Fortinet NSE1, NSE2, and Fortinet Certified Fundamentals in Cybersecurity, Cisco CyberOps Associate, Cybersecurity Essentials, (ISC)² CC, ISO 27001 Internal Auditor, GDPR Foundation, and ISO/IEC 42001 (UKAS) credentials — with CISA preparation ongoing.

My MSc dissertation — "Securing Insurance Companies: A Strategic Approach to ISO 27001 – ISMS Implementation" — reflects my passion for translating standards into real-world resilience. Now in Perth, WA with full working rights, targeting Cybersecurity Management, GRC Consulting, or IS Audit leadership roles.
